# Sales-and-Profit-Dashboard-Advanced-Excel-Business-Intelligence-Project
Sales and Profit Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers, and KPI tracking. The dashboard analyzes sales, profit, customer performance, and regional trends, enabling data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations and business-focused insights.
The Sales and Profit Dashboard is a business-focused analytics solution developed in Microsoft Excel to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making. Designed with a management and stakeholder perspective in mind, the dashboard provides a clear view of business performance through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and trend analysis.

The objective of this project was not only to visualize data but also to answer critical business questions: Which product categories drive the highest revenue? Which customers contribute the most profit? What sales trends exist across regions and time periods? By consolidating multiple dimensions of business data into a single interactive dashboard, decision-makers can quickly identify opportunities, risks, and areas requiring attention.

The dashboard features dynamic Year, Month, and Category slicers, enabling users to drill down into specific business segments without manually manipulating data. Key metrics such as Total Sales and Total Profit provide an executive-level snapshot of organizational performance, while detailed visualizations reveal the underlying drivers behind these results.

Key analytical components include:

Sales and Profit KPIs for performance monitoring.
Profit by Year Analysis to evaluate growth and profitability trends.
Sales by Category to identify top-performing product segments.
Customer Count by Year for understanding customer acquisition patterns.
Top 5 Customers Analysis to support customer retention and revenue optimization strategies.
State-wise Sales Mapping to uncover regional performance trends and market opportunities.
Monthly Sales Trend Analysis to identify seasonality, forecast demand, and improve planning.
